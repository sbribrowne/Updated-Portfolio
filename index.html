HI, I'M SADE.


I am a born and raised New Yorker currently inhabiting the great city of Houston. Between 9-5, I am a Systems Analyst. Any other time I'm a design obsessed web developer feverishly coding my way through one of those bootcamps. My preference for design is clean, simple aesthetics, interactiveness and intuitive UI/UX. What excites me about what I do is that I get to combine two things: my propensity for critical thinking/problem solving and my love for being creative. I get excited about entrepreneurship and bringing new ideas to fruition. As a University of Houston graduate with a Bachelor's in Business Administration and experience in IS and Business Operations, I get the opportunity to bring my business knowledge to the coding table.

Some of my over arching goals include using these magic coding skills to position myself at the intersection of social entreprenership and technology. Becoming an advocate of women participating in the tech space is also important.
